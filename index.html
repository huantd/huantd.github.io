Huan Tran, PhD
Senior Research Scientist, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, USA
Director of Research Innovation, Matmerize, Inc., Atlanta, GA, USA
huan.tran@mse.gatech.edu



My research is motivated by interdisciplinary challenges, both fundamental and real-world, and is driven by synergistic efforts spanning theory, computation, and experimentation in Physics and Materials Science and Engineering. Technically, I am interested in developing innovative methods that integrate theoretical, computational, and artificial-intelligent techniques. Within the emerging paradigm of Materials Informatics, these methods facilitate the accelerated understanding of processing-structure-property relationships and the discovery and design of functional, sustainable materials.
